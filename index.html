Ask About HRD Corp Claims →
What is HRD Corp?
HRD Corp (Human Resource Development Corporation) is Malaysia’s national human capital development agency. Registered employers contribute a levy, which can be claimed back when training is conducted by a registered provider like Marketing Lancers.
What this means for your company
You may be able to reclaim some or all of your training costs through your HRD Corp levy. We guide you through the process — making quality training accessible without straining your training budget.
Our credentials
Marketing Lancers is a fully registered HRD Corp Training Provider with programmes verified as HRD Corp Claimable. Ask us to confirm eligibility for your specific programme during your free discovery call.
